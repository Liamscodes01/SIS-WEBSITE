The PAI Method

Physical Address Intelligence (PAI)

At SIS Verification Services, we do not perform routine background checks. We execute a structured investigative workflow known as the PAI Method, designed to eliminate identity fraud, residency falsification, and guarantor's risk.

Physical Threshold Audit

A trained SIS field agent physically visits the declared residence or workplace to confirm existence, occupancy, and authenticity of claims.

GPS Geo-Fencing

Real-time latitude and longitude coordinates are captured to provide verifiable proof of the exact verification location.

HUM-INT (Human Intelligence)

Discreet neighborhood intelligence is gathered to validate tenure, behavior patterns, and social credibility.

Forensic Reporting

All intelligence is cross-referenced with national identifiers (NIN/BVN) and compiled into an audit-ready Intelligence Dossier.

Why the PAI Method Matters

In environments where identity fraud and misrepresentation are common, the PAI Method ensures every verification is backed by physical evidence, human intelligence, and forensic validation.

This transforms hiring decisions from assumptions into evidence-based certainty.
